Mission and Vision of SRAAA

Vision

"An organization duly recognized by the government and the society by the year 2030, known as the significant contributor in the effective delivery of the schools vision and mission and a provider of avenues for its members holistic development and success."

Mission

"To work side by side with the school and the congregation of the oblates of St Joseph under the principle of cooperation, in the delivery of programs and services which aims to promote positive development of their common stakeholders as a whole."
