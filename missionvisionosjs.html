Mission and Vision of OSJS

Vision of Oblates Schools

Each Oblates of Saint Joseph School, inspired by the Josephite-Marellian spirituality, is a school community producing “virtuous Christians and optimal citizens” and fostering family spirit and Christ-like servant leadership in the society and in the Church.

Mission of Oblates Schools

Each Oblates of Saint Joseph Schools is committed to:

  • participate in the evangelizing mission of the Church;
  • establish a strong community spirit through dialogue;
  • promote Christian virtues and culture of excellence through an integral humanistic education and character formation; and
  • form servant leaders who will advocate the values of family, community, environment, and the Church.
